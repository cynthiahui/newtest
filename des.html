Investment Types for Kids

Stocks

What it is: Imagine owning a tiny piece of your favorite toy company! If it does well, your piece becomes more valuable.

Risk: High (it can go up and down a lot!).

Potential Return: High (if the company becomes super successful!).

Bonds

What it is: It’s like lending your allowance to a friend who promises to give it back with a little extra.

Risk: Low (since they promise to pay you back).

Potential Return: Low (but safe and steady!).

Mutual Funds

What it is: Imagine putting your money together with your friends to buy different toys so no one loses everything if one toy breaks.

Risk: Medium (since it’s a mix of safe and risky things).

Potential Return: Medium (a good balance!).

ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)

What it is: It’s like a basket of different toys that you can trade at any time, just like trading cards!

Risk: Medium (because it holds many things).

Potential Return: Medium (since it's well balanced).
